TITLE: Coworker Overdosing

DATE: Dec 26th, 2023

TAGS: life, fears, drugs, work


A bit of a dreary first entry, but today one of my coworkers came into work overdosing on... something.

Figured I'd write about it since it's still on my mind, and honestly I'm still quite shaken by it.

The guy was usually nice, and really he didn't seem to come off as a user. I was surprised when I turned the corner today and saw him just standing there; lifeless.

Me and a few coworkers had to pull the guy off the kisok and into the kitchen, where I then vigorously rubbed his chest to keep his heart and lungs going.

I feel like I did something, though I'm not sure I did. Coworkers tell me I did so I guess so. Paramedics came, gave narcan, and off he went.

I called around to nearby hospitals to see if he was ok, he was stable thank god, but I was still worried about him.

Drugs are scary fucking things; pray I never get caught up in that.
