WHERE I GET MY SOUNDS

I come from an electronic / synthwave background, listening mainly to creators such as Home, Voyager, Carpenter Brut, etc.

Lyrically I take a lot off of Thom Yorke and Elliot Smith's style of writing, abstract but not without meaning.

A lot of what I do write comes from personal experiences and thoughts, nothing is ingenuine here.
